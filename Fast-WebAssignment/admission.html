Admissions

Admission Criteria

At FAST University, we offer undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across a variety of fields. Admission to our programs is based on merit, as determined by the university's admissions test and the applicant's academic record.

Application Process

  • Submit an online application through the FAST University portal.
  • Pay the application fee via bank transfer or online payment.
  • Appear for the FAST University admission test.
  • Submit required documents for verification (e.g., academic transcripts, test scores).
  • Wait for admission confirmation based on merit.

Important Dates

- Application Deadline: May 31, 2024
- Admission Test: June 10-15, 2024
- Merit List Announcement: July 1, 2024
- Classes Begin: August 15, 2024
