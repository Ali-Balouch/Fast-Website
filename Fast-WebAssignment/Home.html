Welcome to FAST University




































FAST University

FAST University, officially known as the Foundation for Advancement of Science and Technology, is a premier institution of higher education in Pakistan. Established in 2000, the university is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation.

With campuses in Islamabad, Karachi, Lahore, Faisalabad, and Peshawar, FAST University offers a diverse range of undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including business, computer science, and engineering.

Our mission is to foster an environment of intellectual growth, critical thinking, and leadership. We strive to prepare our students to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world and contribute positively to society.

Why FAST?

Founded as a Federally Chartered University in July 2000, the National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences is a premier University of Pakistan, renowned for the quality and impact of its students in the development of local software and other industries.

The university has five modern campuses at Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Peshawar, and Chiniot-Faisalabad. These campuses provide a world-class educational environment and recreational facilities to about over 11,000 students, around one-quarter of whom are female, and over 500 skilled faculty members.

Research wings of the university are well recognized, nationally and internationally. They are embarked upon cutting-edge research having a direct impact on the social, economic, and technological needs of Pakistan.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster an environment of intellectual growth, critical thinking, and leadership. We strive to prepare our students to meet the challenges of a rapidly changing world and contribute positively to society.

Our vision is to become a globally recognized research university of Pakistan within the next decade.
